# Pasadena AI Institute — Brand Guide

> The source of truth for how the Institute looks, sounds, and shows up.
> Hosted by **Whistle Labs**.

---

## 1. The one-liner

**Pasadena AI Institute** — Go from zero to one with AI, in two days, in person, in Pasadena.

## 2. Positioning statement

For professionals, business owners, and executives (especially 35–60) across Pasadena, Greater LA, and Southern California who want to actually *get* AI — the Pasadena AI Institute is a hands-on, in-person, two-day conference that takes you from zero to one. Unlike endless online videos and salesy webinars, we sit with you in a small room until it clicks, and you leave having built something real.

## 3. Name & lockups

- **Full name:** Pasadena AI Institute
- **Tagline / endorsement:** "Hosted by Whistle Labs"
- **Never** abbreviate to "PAII" in customer-facing copy. "Pasadena AI Institute" or "the Institute" is fine.
- Always pair the name with the host on first appearance in a document: *Pasadena AI Institute, hosted by Whistle Labs.*

## 4. Voice & tone

We sound like a sharp, friendly expert who genuinely wants you to succeed — never a hype-machine.

**We are:** warm, clear, confident, encouraging, honest, local.
**We are not:** jargon-heavy, condescending, salesy, breathless, "tech-bro."

Rules of thumb:
- **Plain English always.** If a smart 55-year-old business owner wouldn't instantly get it, rewrite it.
- **Lead with the outcome**, not the technology. ("You'll build something real" > "We cover LLMs and agents.")
- **No fear-mongering.** We don't sell "you'll be left behind." We sell "you're right on time."
- **Be honest about hype.** Naming what's *not* worth their time builds more trust than overselling.
- **Speak to one person.** "You," not "attendees."
- Short sentences. Active voice. Em dashes are fine. Avoid exclamation-point spam.

Signature phrases: *zero to one* · *until it clicks* · *something real* · *in your own backyard* · *no experience required*.

The palette is **deep navy + warm amber/gold on warm off-white** — Craftsman warmth and San Gabriel mountains meet modern, credible AI. Trustworthy for an older professional audience; not cold or generic-tech-blue.

## 6. Typography

- **Display / headings:** Fraunces (serif) — gives "institute" credibility and warmth. Weights 500–700.
- **Body / UI:** Inter (sans) — clean, highly legible. Weights 400–600.
- Fallbacks: Georgia (serif), system-ui (sans).
- Headlines: tight tracking (`-0.01em`), generous size. Body: relaxed line-height (1.6+).

## 7. Logo / mark

An abstract **rising arc over mountains**: the San Gabriel range plus an upward "zero-to-one" trajectory ending in a glowing amber dot (the "one"). Rendered as inline SVG in the site (`components/Logo.tsx`) and as the favicon (`app/icon.svg`). On dark backgrounds, the wordmark goes off-white with an amber "AI."

## 8. Key messaging pillars (use across all channels)

1. **Zero to one** — we start at absolute zero and end with something real.
2. **Hands-on, in person** — a room, not a video. We stay until it clicks.
3. **Two doors, one room** — learn from scratch, *or* build your idea.
4. **Local & community** — proudly Pasadena, serving all of SoCal.
5. **Honest & practical** — what's genuinely useful, minus the hype.

## 9. Proof points / facts to keep consistent

- Format: **2 full days, in person, Pasadena**
- Price: **$995**, all-inclusive (materials, lunches, software setup, alumni community)
- First cohort: **Founding Cohort, Fall 2026**, limited to **24 seats**
- Two tracks: **Zero Experience** and **I Have an Idea**
- Tools set up: **Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor**, AI agents, vibe coding, custom workflows
- Host: **Whistle Labs** (whistlelabs.ai)

## 10. Calls to action (canonical wording)

- Primary (ready to buy): **"Reserve your seat — $995"**
- Secondary (needs nurture): **"Apply / ask a question"** → interest form → Zoom call
- Never use generic "Sign up" or "Learn more" as the only CTA.

## 11. Do / Don't quick list

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| "No experience required." | "For beginners and dummies." |
| "You'll build something real." | "Unlock the power of AI!!!" |
| "Right on time." | "Don't get left behind." |
| "Until it clicks." | "Master AI in 48 hours." |
| Name the actual tools. | Vague "cutting-edge technology." |
