# Email Templates — Pasadena AI Institute

Ready-to-use, copy-paste email copy for the Founding Cohort funnel. Each file has a
**Subject:** line, a plain-text body, and usage notes at the top (in an HTML comment)
explaining when to send it and what to personalize.

> **Before you send anything:** fill in every `[bracketed]` placeholder
> (e.g. `[Name]`, `[their company]`, `[date]`, `[venue]`, `[payment link]`, `[your name]`).
> These emails are written to be sent **personally** from your own email client — read them
> once before hitting send so they sound like you.

## The funnel, in order

| # | File | When to send | Goal |
|---|------|--------------|------|
| 1 | `01-cold-outreach.md` | First touch to a local business owner / org leader | Introduce the Institute, invite them (or their team/members) |
| 2 | `02-interest-followup.md` | Right after someone submits the interest form | Book a 15-minute Zoom/phone call |
| 3 | `03-post-zoom-payment.md` | Same day as the call | Recap fit, send the reserve/payment link, gentle real urgency |
| 4 | `04-payment-confirmation.md` | Right after they pay via Stripe | Warm welcome, set expectations for what's next |
| 5 | `05-pre-conference-prep.md` | ~1 week before the conference | Logistics, what to bring, accounts to create, what to expect |
| 6 | `06-post-conference.md` | 1–2 days after the conference | Thank them, request testimonial, referral incentive, next cohort |
| 7 | `07-waitlist-nurture.md` | For not-yet-ready / waitlisted folks | 2-email nurture (Email A + Email B), value-first |

## Two paths into the funnel

- **Ready to buy:** pays via Stripe on the site → **#4** → **#5** → **#6**
- **Needs nurture:** interest form → **#2** → Zoom call → **#3** → (pays) → **#4** → **#5** → **#6**
- **Not ready yet:** drops into **#7** (Email A, then Email B). If they re-engage, move them back to **#2/#3**.

## Voice reminder

Warm, clear, confident, plain-English. Lead with the outcome. No fear-mongering, no hype.
Speak to one person ("you"). See `../BRAND.md` for the full guide.
